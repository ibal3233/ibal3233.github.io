About me

I am a researcher at the Intelligent Innovation G5-AICT Research Center, Chonnam National University. I was a postdoc researcher in the same school at department of Intelligent Electronic and Computer Engineering. Prevously, I received the B.Eng. and M.Eng. degrees in computer engineering from the Federal University of Technology, Minna, Nigeria, in 2014 and 2018, respectively, and the Ph.D. degree in computer science and engineering from Chonnam National University, Gwangju, South Korea, in 2022.

My Postdoc research focuses on the design of the segment routing (SR)-based in-network computing (COIN) architecture for the Metaverse. The main goal is to leverage segment routing (SR) and COIN to build a sustainable network and metaverse ecosystems. Leveraging these technologies would support the immersive network requirement through advanced in-network programming, distributed network orchestration, resource discovery and secure computation from source to destination with faster rerouting and traffic engineering guarantees.

The dissertation addresses enhancing network security in smart cities, focusing on using citizen-generated data with strict confidentiality and resilience against AI adversarial threats.
It explores three critical research questions:

  • Developing privacy-preserving IDS training algorithms
  • Leveraging big data to bolster IDS resilience
  • Evaluating the threat of adversarial examples in federated learning-based IDS in smart cities

Research Interest

My research focuses on AI/ML for networking, AI, digital twin network, in-network computing(COIN). My previous work focused on harnessing the benefits of COIN to build a sustainable network and metaverse ecosystems. Leveraging these technologies would support the immersive network requirement through advanced in-network programming, distributed network orchestration, resource discovery and secure computation from source to destination with faster rerouting and traffic engineering guarantees.

My other research focuses on the efficient harnessing of knowledge from huge data generated in the smart city to build a resilient AI (Artificial Intelligent) model through the use of technology that will encourage the citizens to participate in the training process by offering the data they generated while preserving the confidentiality of their data.

In this vast areas, my research is at the intersection of AI/ML, network systems and digital twin.
